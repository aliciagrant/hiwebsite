about me

I am a third-year Computer Science student at the University of Florida with an interdisciplinary focus in Psychology, aspiring to grow in user experience design and product management. My goal is to enhance my UI/UX, technical, and product strategy skill sets through meaningful opportunities.

The "skip intro" button, after hours of binge-watching TV, sparked more than just a moment of appreciation—it was a realization. This small feature inspired me to pursue UX design, showing me how impactful and thoughtful design can improve everyday experiences. Now, I'm dedicated to making strides in a field where I can blend technology, creativity, and empathy.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I enjoy reading, writing music, and spending quality time with my little sister and friends.
